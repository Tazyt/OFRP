About Operation Freedom

Unleashing tactical immersion and military precision in the world of FiveM RP.

What is Operation Freedom?

Operation Freedom Roleplay is a high-intensity, military-inspired FiveM server built for players who crave structured roleplay, immersive storytelling, and elite tactical operations. Our server is designed with realism and brotherhood in mind—every mission, patrol, and op matters.

Our Core Values

  • Realism: Authentic military-style roleplay experiences.
  • Respect: A welcoming, mature community where all players are valued.
  • Professionalism: Staff and leadership conduct themselves with integrity and discipline.
  • Immersion: Every encounter is part of the story—your role matters.

Features

  • Custom military base and operations hub
  • Unique missions and contracts
  • Whitelisted elite units
  • Realistic weapon handling and tactical gear
  • Rank progression and specialized training
