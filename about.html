About Operation Freedom

Unleashing tactical immersion and military precision in the world of FiveM RP.

What is Operation Freedom?

Operation Freedom Roleplay is an 18+ Whitelisted FiveM server where players create unforgettable stories. Founded in honor of fallen U.S. Armed Forces members and emergency responders, our community is built on respect, integrity, and unity. We welcome everyone—regardless of background—to a place where you're valued and free to be yourself. If you’re ready for authentic bonds, tactical gameplay, and powerful roleplay, Operation Freedom awaits.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Custom Scripts: Built for deep immersion and unique interactions.
  • Extensive Vehicle Fleet: Over 1,000 vehicles for unmatched realism.
  • Custom MLOs: Immersive, handcrafted interiors and detailed environments.
  • Community-Driven: Evolving features shaped by player feedback and innovation.

Features
