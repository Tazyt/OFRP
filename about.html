What is Operation Freedom?

Operation Freedom Roleplay is a 18+ Whitelisted FiveM server not only for people to come in and create stories and memories with friends and maybe even family operation freedom roleplay was created to honor our fallen heros of the U.S Armed Forces and other Emergancy Services from veterans who died serving this country with unmatched strength and passion for ol glory bleeding for our freedoms making sure we as a country is safe from harm operation freedom is and always be a home to all to those in need of a place where they are respected and loved regardless of race, religion, color, creed or sexual orientation all is welcomed with open arms so come on down to operation freedom roleplay if you think you can handle the pressure of creating a bond with someone and being true to who you are.
